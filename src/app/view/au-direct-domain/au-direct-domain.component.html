.au Direct Domain

.au direct domain names, e.g., domain.au, getyour.au, etc. – are available as a new option for Internet users from March 2022.
.au direct domain extension is general purpose. Anyone with a verified connection to Australia can get it to manage their online presence for themselves or their organization.

If you already hold a domain name in any other .au namespace, e.g., com.au, org.au, etc. you can apply for Priority Status to register the exact match in .au direct. However, the Priority Status is only available for the first six months from launch.

To check your priority category and/or see if there are other registrants eligible for the .au direct name you seek, you can use auDA’s Priority Status Tool.

Popular domain registrars have already enabled purchase of au direct. You can try your favorite registrar to get it.

Many Australians are referring to it as a money grab, whilst domainers seem quite happy.

Now that au direct is here, it is a small price to pay to secure your brand if you are already using au namespace.
