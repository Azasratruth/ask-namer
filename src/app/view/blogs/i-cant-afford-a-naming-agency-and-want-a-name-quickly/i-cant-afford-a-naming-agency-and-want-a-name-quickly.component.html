I can’t afford a naming agency and want a name for my business quickly

Simple answer – Name Generators
There are numerous name generators on the internet. They all work more or less on the same algorithm. Hence the results might be the same from two generators. One noteworthy thing about name generators is that they only produce descriptive names, i.e., names based on the function of the service/product.
